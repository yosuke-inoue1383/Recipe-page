Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth clasic cheesecake recipe! Paired with a buttery graham cracker crust,no one can deny its simple decadence. For the best results, bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet
    graham crackers)
  • 5 Tablespoons (70g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
Cheesecake
  • four 8-ounce blocks (904g) full-fat cream cheese, softened to room
    temperature
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • YIELDS
    12 servings
  • PREP TIME
    45 minutes
  • COOK TIME
    1 hour
  • TOTAL TIME
    7.75hours
